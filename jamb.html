How to Check Your JAMB PIN (ePIN) in 2025 — Step-by-Step (Rank #1 Target)

If you’re searching for “how to check JAMB PIN” or “how to check JAMB ePIN”, follow these verified steps. This is one of the most common problems candidates face every year.

1) Check SMS from 55019 or 66019

When you generate your profile code or complete ePIN purchase, JAMB (or the vendor) sends you an SMS:


NIN 12******01 -- send to 55019 or 66019
You will receive your Profile Code via SMS.
Use it to buy your JAMB ePIN.

2) Check Your Email Inbox

If you bought your ePIN online (Remita, Interswitch, Quickteller, etc.), they usually email a receipt containing your ePIN. Search your inbox for: “JAMB ePIN”, “UTME ePIN”, or the vendor’s name.

3) Check Your Payment Receipt (Bank, POS, Online)

The receipt given to you during payment almost always shows the ePIN. If you’ve misplaced it, return to the bank or POS vendor for a reprint.

4) Log in to JAMB eFacility Portal

  1. Go to efacility.jamb.gov.ng
  2. Log in with your email & password.
  3. Check your payment history for the ePIN details.

5) Visit an Accredited JAMB CBT Centre

Still can’t find it? A CBT centre can help recover your ePIN using your profile code, phone number, or NIN.

Scam Alert: Never share your ePIN/Profile Code/NIN with Agents on social media. Always use official vendors and accredited CBT centres.

Lost Your JAMB ePIN or Profile Code? (Recovery Options)

  • Profile Code: Re-send NIN + Your NIN to 55019 or 66019.
  • ePIN: Check SMS, email, payment receipt, or CBT center.
  • Wrong phone number? Visit a CBT centre for corrections.
  • Still stuck? Use the official JAMB support channels.

Step-by-Step JAMB 2025 Registration Guide (UTME & DE)

  1. Get NIN: Mandatory for JAMB 2025 — enroll at NIMC centres.
  2. Generate Profile Code: Text NIN + Your NIN to 55019/66019.
  3. Buy JAMB ePIN: ₦5,700 (UTME) / ₦6,200 (DE) via bank, POS, Remita, Interswitch, Quickteller, etc.
  4. Register at CBT Centre: Biometrics, course/school selection, O’Level upload.
  5. Print e-Slip: Exam date, time & venue. Don’t lose it.

Print and keep copies of every document (receipt, e-slip, change of course printout, etc.).

JAMB CAPS 2025 – Accepting or Rejecting Admission

  1. Log in to efacility.jamb.gov.ng.
  2. Click “Check Admission Status”.
  3. Open CAPS and monitor offers.
  4. Click Accept or Reject promptly.

If you do not accept in time, the school can offer it to another candidate.

How to Check JAMB Results (SMS & Portal)

Via SMS

  • Text RESULT to 55019 or 66019 (₦50 charge applies).

Via Portal

  1. Visit efacility.jamb.gov.ng.
  2. Log in → Click “Check UTME Result”.

JAMB Data Correction (Name, DOB, Course/Institution, Gender, LGA, etc.)

FAQs About JAMB 2025

How do I check my JAMB PIN (ePIN) for 2025?

Check SMS from 55019/66019, your email receipt, your payment slip, your JAMB eFacility portal, or visit a CBT centre for recovery.

What’s the difference between JAMB Profile Code and JAMB ePIN?

Profile Code is generated via SMS using your NIN; ePIN is purchased using that profile code and is needed to complete registration at a CBT centre.

When will JAMB 2025 registration start and end?

Expected January 15 – February 25, 2025. JAMB will officially confirm the dates.

How much is JAMB 2025 registration?

₦5,700 for UTME, ₦6,200 for DE (subject to official announcement).

What is the JAMB 2025 cut-off mark?

Universities: 140–200; Polytechnics: 100–120; Colleges of Education: 100. Top schools use higher internal cut-offs.

What is the cut-off mark for UNILAG 2025?

General cut-off is typically 200+, but Medicine, Law, and Nursing may require 240–260+.

How do I retrieve my JAMB profile code?

Resend NIN + your 11-digit NIN to 55019 or 66019 with the same phone number used originally.

Can I register JAMB without NIN?

No. NIN is compulsory for JAMB 2025 registration.

Is JAMB mock exam compulsory?

No, but it is strongly recommended to understand the CBT system.

How do I check my JAMB result?

Via SMS: text RESULT to 55019/66019; via portal: efacility.jamb.gov.ng.

What is JAMB CAPS?

CAPS is the Central Admission Processing System where you accept or reject admission.

Can I change my course or institution after UTME?

Yes. Use the Change of Course/Institution service at accredited CBT centres.

Can I combine WAEC and NECO?

Yes, most schools accept two sittings, but some courses (like Medicine/Law) may not. Confirm with your school.

How many times can I write JAMB?

Unlimited. You can take it as many times as needed.

How much is JAMB data correction?

Usually ₦3,000 (may vary). Processed at accredited CBT centres.

When will JAMB 2025 results be out?

Typically 48–72 hours after each candidate’s exam date.

What JAMB score do I need for scholarships?

Typically 280+ with strong O’Level results; depends on scholarship requirements.

What happens if I forget my email password for JAMB?

Reset it via your email provider, or use your phone number on the eFacility page if supported. As a last resort, visit a CBT centre.

Can I re-use last year’s JAMB ePIN?

No. Each exam cycle requires a fresh ePIN linked to your profile code for that year.
