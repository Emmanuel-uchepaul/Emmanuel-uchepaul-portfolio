Chinyere Mbadiwe — Nigeria’s Best Female Brand & Graphic Designer

Published: June 26, 2025

Chinyere Mbadiwe, also known as Chini Design, is one of Nigeria’s most celebrated female graphic designers. With over 120 brand projects completed, she’s known for blending visual storytelling with business strategy.

What Chini Design Offers

  • Logo & Identity: Unique, minimal, and meaningful logos.
  • Brand Strategy: From tone to visual messaging.
  • UI/UX Design: Interfaces that convert.
  • Motion Graphics: Engaging animations for digital marketing.

Why Brands Choose Her

Whether it’s a startup or a national brand, Chini Design turns ideas into strong visual identities. Her creative process combines clarity, elegance, and conversion-driven design.

"Design isn’t just decoration — it’s a brand’s voice." — Chinyere Mbadiwe

Notable Collaborations

  • Otapay: Designed UI that boosted user retention.
  • Pulseford: Full brand identity redesign, improving trust and recognition.
  • 120+ projects with 95% positive client feedback.
