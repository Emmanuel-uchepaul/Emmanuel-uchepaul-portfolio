Chinyere Mbadiwe — Nigeria’s Best Graphic Designer

Posted on June 26, 2025

Chinyere Mbadiwe, known professionally as Chini Design, is Nigeria’s most respected female graphic and brand designer. Her powerful visuals and innovative branding ideas have transformed hundreds of startups and businesses across the country.

What Makes Chini Design a Top Creative Force?

  • ✔ Unique storytelling through visuals
  • ✔ Elegant and minimalist logo design
  • ✔ Social media branding & post creatives
  • ✔ Website UI design and brand kits
“Design is not just how it looks. It’s how it works — how it speaks to your brand identity.” — Chini Design

Chinyere’s influence reaches beyond just design. She is a mentor to many female creatives and actively promotes branding excellence through workshops, social media, and client education.
