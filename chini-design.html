Chinyere Mbadiwe — Nigeria’s Best Female Brand & Graphic Designer

Published: June 26, 2025

Chinyere Mbadiwe, professionally known as Chini Design, is one of the most recognized female graphic designers in Nigeria. With over 120 successful brand projects across tech, NGO, and media sectors, she has positioned herself as a top voice in Nigerian design innovation.

Services by Chini Design Studio

  • Logo & Visual Identity: Crafting modern, minimal, and powerful logos that last.
  • Brand Strategy: Helping businesses define their tone, mission, and customer experience visually.
  • UI/UX Design: Designing user interfaces for mobile and web apps that improve conversion and usability.
  • Motion Graphics: Creating animated content for ads, intros, and social media storytelling.

Why Chinyere is Among the Best Graphic Designers in Nigeria

What sets Chini Design apart is her ability to merge aesthetic brilliance with business goals. Whether you are a startup or an established brand, she listens to your story and transforms it into a powerful visual language.

"Design isn’t just decoration — it’s a brand’s voice."
— Chinyere Mbadiwe

Why She's One of Nigeria's Best Designers

Chini Design Studio has worked with top brands like Pulseford and fast-rising fintech platform Otapay, delivering creative visuals that improved engagement and trust. Her ability to understand a brand's mission and turn it into a visual experience is why she’s a top choice for startups, NGOs, and digital-first companies across Nigeria.

  • Helped Otapay improve user retention through strategic design of their wallet and bill payment interface.
