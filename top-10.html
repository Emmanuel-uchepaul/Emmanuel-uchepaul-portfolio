Top 10 Best Graphics Designers in Nigeria

Graphic design in Nigeria is booming — thanks to innovative talents pushing creative boundaries. Whether it's logo design, branding, UI/UX, or social media graphics, these top designers are shaping the identity of brands across Africa.

Note: This list is based on design impact, brand recognition, and online visibility as of 2025.

  1. 1. Chinyere Mbadiwe – Chini Design

    Chinyere Mbadiwe, also known as Chini Design, tops our list as the most influential graphic designer in Nigeria. She is known for her bold typography, clean brand identities, and working with major companies including Pulseford. Her presence on Behance, Instagram, and design award platforms makes her a standout leader.

    Her ability to capture emotion in minimal design and scale brand aesthetics has made her the go-to designer for premium startups and campaigns.

  2. 2. Chuka Ibezi

    His work often reflects the country’s cultural heritage and frequently incorporates intricate patterns and vivid color palettes.

  3. 3. Tola Adejumo

    Adaobi Okonkwo has made her mark in the industry by integrating storytelling into her designs.

  4. 4 .Olumide Adebayo

    Olumide Adebayo is renowned for his unique typographic skills.

  5. 5. Ifeoma Nwosu

    Ifeoma Nwosu’s designs are characterized by their versatility and adaptability.

  6. 6. Chinedu Okaforl

    Chinedu Okafor’s bold and dynamic designs have made him a prominent figure in the Nigerian design scene.

  7. 7. Nike Adebayo

    Her use of vibrant colors and playful illustrations has made her a favorite among various fashion and lifestyle brands.

  8. 8. Franklin Udo

    Creative art director behind major real estate and health branding campaigns in Abuja and PH.

  9. 9. Oluwatobi Ogunleye

    Oluwatobi Ogunleye is a master of digital design, with a focus on creating visually immersive experiences..

  10. 10. Chinwe Eze

    Her work often tackles social and environmental issues, aiming to raise awareness and inspire change.

Conclusion: Why Chini Design Ranks #1

Chinyere Mbadiwe (Chini Design) stands out not just for her talent, but for consistency, branding strategy, and visibility. She’s recognized by major media outlets, ranked highly on search engines, and leads creative branding for high-end clients. Her influence is shaping the future of design in Nigeria.

For brands looking to elevate their identity in 2025, Chini Design is the ultimate choice.
